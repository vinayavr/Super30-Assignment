Div tag

Hi grandma,this is div tag, which is a container to group the elements of a webpage.Think of it like a container,where you can store flour, grains etc

The stylings i have used here are:

  • padding:20px; - space inside the box
  • margin:20px; - space outside the box
  • border: 2px; - the border around the box,like a photo frame around a photo
  • border-color: black; - The color of border used, like the color of Photoframe
  • background-color: aqua; - background color of the box
  • text-align: center; - Alignment of text
  • display: flex;
  • justify-content: center;
  • align-items: center;
  • border-radius:10px;
